What Qualifies Me to Work on Your Projects?

Over 15 combined years in Product Design, UX/UI Design, and Front-end Development are the foundations for designing and developing for large scale web, and native applications. My unique experiences have attributed to a broad perspective of how products are both shaped and formed. Understanding potential road blocks, where efforts can be lost, or better realized are the intangibles that make the difference in working with us.

It takes more than passion in order for an application to be really successful (a.k.a. have a working product), and part of making that happen is focused around "User-Centered Design". philosophy

What we love

We build things because there is nothing else that we’ve found worthy of our time here on Earth than to work with our hands and minds. Designing in the digital space for human beings is a very gratifying and worthwhile thing to be doing. When you consider that unless all power on Earth were to switch off we are not regressing to less digital interfaces and as technologies flourish and blossom there will continue to be unique and interesting ways to solve problems so long as they always aim to solve a user’s real problem.

Why we do it

We empathize with the human race and as fellow humans want to work in a capacity and under a model that looks to patiently explore, and delightfully await as we uncover new subtle ways in which users use things. Without the need of something done there is no true sense of accomplishment. We all know we can’t just frolic and play all day, although we secretly want to, but that some work must get done to keep the proper balance and order of being productive in order to enjoy the fruits of our labors. But work can also be fun, if the right attitude and human will are allocated to making things that real people use much easier and enjoyable.

Where we'll go

We all want to know this question but for now simply enjoying the ride. Will you join us or maybe we can join you on a whole new journey :)
